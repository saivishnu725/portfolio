03 : About me

Who am I?

I am a small student out of no where trying to learn programming and having a online documentation of my progress.

Why am I documenting it?

I heard someone say that "Imagine going to someone and saying that you can fly and how do you expect them to believe you if you can't show it to them." and the same hit me. What if I say someone reaches me for my skills and then I have nothing to present my skills in front of them? Now the solution was having a portfolio to showcase myself.

I plan on trying to keep this website as updated as possible by constantly adding new projects a small article on each of them whenever time permits me.
